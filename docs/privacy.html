Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Information We Collect

RYDL is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide you with the best gaming experience:

  • Game Progress: Your scores, achievements, and game statistics are stored locally on your device
  • Account Information: If you choose to create an account, we collect your email address for authentication
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics to help us improve the game experience

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the game service
  • Improve game features and user experience
  • Communicate important updates or changes
  • Ensure fair play and prevent cheating

Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely. Game progress is primarily stored on your device, giving you full control over your data.

Third-Party Services

RYDL may use third-party services for analytics and crash reporting to improve app stability and performance. These services are configured to respect your privacy and collect only anonymized data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of analytics collection
  • Update your account information at any time

Children's Privacy

RYDL is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via the app or email.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@rydl.app
