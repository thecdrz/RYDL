üéÆ How to Play RYDL

Basic Rules

RYDL is a number puzzle game where you crack a 5-digit code in 6 tries or less.

Game Modes

  • Classic - Take your time, 6 guesses to crack the code
  • Speed - Race against the clock (2 minutes!)
  • Hard - No yellow hints, only green or gray
  • Unique - All digits are different, no repeats
  • Practice - Unlimited guesses to master your skills

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play with friends?
Go to the Multiplayer tab, add friends by username, then challenge them to custom puzzles! You can also share your results after completing a game.
How do daily challenges work?
Every day you get 3 new challenges with specific goals (like "complete in under 2 minutes" or "solve in 3 tries"). Complete them to earn bonus points!
Can I change the theme?
Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Themes and choose from 8 beautiful themes including Neon, Sunset, Ocean, Forest, Retro, Pastel, and Cyberpunk. Dark mode is also available!
Does RYDL work offline?
Yes! All solo game modes work completely offline. You only need internet for multiplayer features like leaderboards and friend challenges.
Are there any ads or subscriptions?
No! RYDL is completely free with zero ads and no subscriptions. All features are unlocked from the start. We respect your time and attention.
How do I turn off sounds or haptics?
Open the Settings screen (tap the gear icon) and toggle Sound Effects, Ambient Music, or Haptics on/off as you prefer.
How are achievements unlocked?
Play games to unlock 20+ achievements! Check your progress in the Stats screen. Achievements range from common (first win) to legendary (perfect game in 1 guess).
Can I reset my statistics?
Yes, go to Settings ‚Üí Reset Statistics. This will clear your game history, streaks, and stats. This action cannot be undone!
How do I add friends?
Go to the Leaderboard screen ‚Üí Friends tab ‚Üí Add Friend. Search for their username and send a friend request. They'll receive a notification.
What data does RYDL collect?
Very minimal! We only collect your username, game statistics, and friend connections (stored via Firebase). No personal information, no tracking, no ads. See our Privacy Policy for details.

üîß Troubleshooting

App is crashing or freezing

  • Make sure you have the latest version of RYDL from the App Store
  • Restart your iPhone/iPad
  • Check that you have iOS 13.0 or later
  • If the problem persists, contact us at support@RYDL.app

Friend challenges not working

  • Check your internet connection
  • Make sure both you and your friend have the latest app version
  • Try refreshing the Multiplayer screen (pull down to refresh)
  • Restart the app

Can't see my stats or progress

  • Your stats are saved locally on your device
  • If you reinstalled the app, stats may have been reset
  • Make sure you didn't accidentally tap "Reset Statistics" in Settings

Themes not applying

  • Close and reopen the app
  • Make sure you tapped "Apply" after selecting a theme
  • Check that you're running the latest version

üí° Tips & Tricks

Strategy Tips

  • Start with a guess that has different digits (like 12345) to get maximum information
  • Pay attention to yellow circles - that digit IS in the code, just in a different spot
  • Use the process of elimination - gray digits are not in the code at all
  • In Unique mode, no digit repeats, so use this to your advantage
  • In Hard mode, there are no yellow hints - only green or gray!

Maximize Your Score

  • Win in fewer guesses to earn more points
  • Complete daily challenges for bonus points
  • Build a win streak for achievement unlocks
  • Try Speed mode for an adrenaline rush!

Social Features

  • Challenge friends to beat your time after completing a puzzle
  • Share your results to social media after a win
  • Check the leaderboard to see where you rank
  • Use the "Invite Friends" button to grow your friend list
