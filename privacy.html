Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 24, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to RYDL ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Username: You choose a username to identify yourself in the game
  • Game Statistics: Your game scores, win/loss records, streaks, and gameplay history
  • Social Data: Friend connections, challenge history, and leaderboard participation

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Game sessions, feature usage, and app performance data
  • Firebase Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our app

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the game functionality
  • Enable multiplayer features and friend connections
  • Display leaderboards and track your progress
  • Improve game performance and user experience
  • Send push notifications (if you opt-in)
  • Detect and prevent technical issues

Data Storage and Security

  • Firebase: We use Google Firebase to securely store user data, including usernames, game statistics, and social connections
  • Encryption: Data is transmitted using secure HTTPS connections
  • Retention: Your data is retained as long as your account is active

Sharing of Information

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes

We MAY share information:

  • With Firebase/Google services to provide app functionality
  • If required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • In the event of a business transfer or acquisition

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of push notifications
  • Reset your statistics

Children's Privacy

RYDL is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and analytics - Privacy Policy
  • Expo: App development framework and push notifications - Privacy Policy

Push Notifications

If you opt-in to push notifications, we may send:

  • Friend challenge notifications
  • Game result updates
  • Daily puzzle reminders

You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices:

Email: support@RYDL.app
